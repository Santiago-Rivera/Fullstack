The list elements

There are three types of lists in HTML:

  • Ordered List: A list of items in a specific order, typically numbered.
  • Unordered List: A list of items without a specific order, typically bulleted.
  • Description List: A list of terms and their descriptions.

Ordered List Example

  1. First Me
  2. Second Mom
  3. Third Father

Unordered List Example

  • First Father
  • Second Mom
  • Third Me

Ejercicio 1

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • ¼ cup butter

For the filling:

  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  2. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  4. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  5. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  6. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
  8. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  9. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
  10. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
